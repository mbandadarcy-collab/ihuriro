About IHURIRO

Connecting employers with trusted household workers

What is IHURIRO?

IHURIRO is a platform that connects employers with household workers in Rwanda. We help people find reliable workers with clear and verified information.

Our Goal

Our goal is to make it easier for employers to find household workers in a safe, reliable, and respectful way that honors everyone's rights.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Clear and verified worker information
  • Worker selection based on categories (live-in, live-out, gender, languages)
  • An easy-to-use platform on computers and mobile phones
  • Respect for security and data privacy

How It Works

An employer can search for a worker using categories, review key information, and then register to gain a way to communicate with the selected worker.
