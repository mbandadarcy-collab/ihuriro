Amategeko n’Amabwiriza

1. Intego y’uru rubuga:
Urubuga rwacu rugufasha kubona abakozi bo mu rugo bizewe mu buryo bwihuse kandi butekanye. Ukoresha uru rubuga wemera amategeko n’amabwiriza ari hano.

2. Uruhare rwa Platform:
Platform ihuza gusa abakozi n’abakoresha. Ntabwo dutunganya imishahara, amasezerano, cyangwa imicungire ya buri munsi. Ibyemezo byose bijyanye n’akazi bifatwa hagati y’umukozi n’umukoresha.

3. Kwiyandikisha & Amakuru:
Abakozi bagomba gutanga amakuru y’ukuri kandi yuzuye. Abakoresha bagomba gukoresha aya makuru mu buryo bukwiriye. Gutanga amakuru atari yo bishobora gutuma konti isubikwa.

4. Ibikoresho Byatanzwe:
Abakozi bemera ko ibikoresho byatanzwe ari ukuri. Platform ishobora kwanga, gusiba, cyangwa gusubika konti zifite amakuru atari yo.

5. Uburenganzira ku Gusangiza Amakuru:
Amakuru y’umukozi ashobora gusangizwa abakoresha kugira ngo bafate ibyemezo byubakiye ku makuru, harimo amazina, nimero za telefone, n’ibikoresho byatanzwe.

6. Kwerekana Nimero za Telefone:
Nimero za telefone z’abakozi ntizizagaragara kugeza igihe kwishyura byizewe bikorwa hifashishijwe Flutterwave.

7. Uko Kwishyura Bikora:
Amafaranga yose ashyirwaho na platform. Kwishyura ntibisubizwa keretse habayeho ikosa rya platform.

8. Inshingano z’Abakoresha:
Abakoresha bagomba gukoresha platform mu buryo bwubahiriza amategeko. Gukoresha nabi bishobora gutuma konti isubikwa cyangwa ihagarikwa burundu.

9. Politiki y’Ubwirinzi bw’Amakuru:
Amakuru yose y’abakoresha abikwa mu buryo butekanye kandi akoreshwa gusa mu guhuza abakozi n’abakoresha. Amakuru ntagurishwa cyangwa ngo asangirwe nta burenganzira bw’umukoresha.

10. Impinduka ku Mategeko:
Aya mategeko ashobora guhindurwa igihe icyo ari cyo cyose. Gukomeza gukoresha platform bivuze ko wemeye impinduka zose.

11. Uburyo bwo Kuvugana:
Ku bibazo cyangwa imikoro, hamagara platform ukoresheje amakuru ari ku rubuga.
