Ibyerekeye IHURIRO

Guhuza abakoresha n’abakozi bo mu rugo bafite icyizere

IHURIRO ni iki?

IHURIRO ni urubuga ruhuza abakoresha n’abakozi bo mu rugo mu Rwanda. Dufasha abantu kubona abakozi bizewe, bafite amakuru asobanutse kandi yizewe.

Intego yacu

Intego yacu ni koroshya uburyo abakoresha babona abakozi bo mu rugo, mu buryo butekanye, bwizewe kandi burimo kubaha uburenganzira bwa buri wese.

Icyo dutandukaniyeho

  • Amakuru y’abakozi asobanutse kandi yemejwe
  • Kugena abakozi hakurikijwe ibyiciro (uba mu rugo, utaha, igitsina, indimi)
  • Urubuga rworoshye gukoresha ku mudasobwa no kuri telefoni
  • Kubaha umutekano n’ibanga ry’amakuru

Uko bikora

Umukoresha ashobora gushakisha umukozi akoresheje ibyiciro, akareba amakuru y’ingenzi, hanyuma akiyandikisha kugira ngo abone uburyo bwo kuvugana n’umukozi wahisemo.
